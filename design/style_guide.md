# Style Guide – UWA Community App

## Colors
- Primary: Blue (#1E3A8A)
- Background: Light blue
- Cards: White / light grey

## Layout
- Simple and clean layout
- Grid-based structure
- Cards used for posts and products

## Navbar
- Top navigation bar
- Links: Home, Forum, Marketplace
- Profile/Login on right side

## Buttons
- Rounded or rectangular buttons
- Light background with blue border

## Pages Designed
- Homepage
- Login Page
- Forum Page
- Marketplace Page
