# Project Overview

## Project Description
Our project is a campus-based community web application designed specifically for UWA students. The platform combines several useful student-focused functions in one place, including lost and found posts, second-hand marketplace listings, club and society pages, and general community interaction through posts and comments.

The application will work like a student forum where users can create text or image posts, comment on other posts, and vote using upvotes or downvotes. To keep the platform relevant and safe for the university community, access will be restricted to approved users, preferably through a UWA email address.

## Purpose of the App
The purpose of this app is to create a simple and useful online space for students within the UWA community. It aims to help students reconnect with lost items, buy and sell used goods conveniently, engage with student clubs and societies, and interact with each other through community discussions.

By bringing these functions together into one platform, the app makes campus communication more organised, accessible, and helpful for students.

## Target Users
The main target users of this app are UWA students.

More specifically, the app is designed for:
- students who want to report lost or found items
- students who want to buy or sell second-hand goods
- students who want to interact with campus clubs and societies
- students who want to participate in a university-only online community

Because the platform is intended for the school community only, users will need to sign up with an approved account, such as a UWA email address.

## Key Features
The initial feature list for the app includes:
1. Student login and signup
2. Access restricted to approved users or UWA email holders
3. Text and image posts
4. Lost and Found section
5. Comments on posts
6. Upvotes and downvotes
7. Club and society pages
8. Marketplace for used items

## App Summary
This app is a student-only campus community platform for UWA. It allows students to create posts, interact with others, report lost and found items, trade used goods, and connect with clubs and societies in one central website.
