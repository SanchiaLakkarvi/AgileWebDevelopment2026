Welcome to GuildSpace

A student-only UWA community platform for lost property, second-hand trading, and campus interaction.

Explore Community Sign Up

Built for UWA students

GuildSpace is a student-only platform where users can post about lost and found items, buy and sell second-hand goods, and connect through a simple community forum.

The platform is intended to be accessible, useful, and relevant for the university community.

What makes it useful?
  • UWA student-focused community
  • Lost & Found support
  • Marketplace for used items
  • Forum-style interaction

Main Features

Everything students need in one place

Lost & Found

Post lost or found items and help students reconnect with their belongings.

Marketplace

Buy and sell books, electronics, and useful items around campus.

Forum

Share posts, ask questions, and connect with the student community.

Secure Access

Access is limited to approved users, helping keep the platform campus-focused.
